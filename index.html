Team eStars!


Who we are:  eStars is an e-sports event management firm, organising innovative events and providing a holistic customer experience. We lay emphasis on and craft each and every aspect of the event with utmost diligence, gaming being at the heart of it.

What we do:   We design memorable experiences for our customers, and provide a variety that appeals to everyone's interests. We aim at building an interconnected entertainment network hich changes the perception of gaming, and brings out the suppressed talents of the millennials.

Why us:   The e-sports industry, in today's digital world, is in constant motion. There are a multitude companies organising events in this area. However, they mostly cater to professional gamers, who can showcase their skills and win the competition. Our goals are different; we provide a platform for people to enjoy gaming and cherish various other allied aspects, instead of just being part of a 'race'.
